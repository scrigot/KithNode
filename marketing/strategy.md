# KithNode Marketing Strategy (Phase 0, pre-launch)

*Living source of truth. Last updated 2026-06-15. Pairs with `.agents/product-marketing.md`.*

## The frame
KithNode is pre-beta. Marketing now is NOT conversion or advertising. The job is to build a small OWNED audience (email waitlist) and a repeatable, delegatable content engine, so launch day starts from a list instead of zero.

## Two principles
1. Every channel funnels to ONE owned asset: the email waitlist. Followers are rented; emails are owned.
2. Build the system, not the posts. One idea, many formats, batched weekly, tracked, handoff-ready.

## Identity posture
- KithNode = brand account (TikTok now, IG later).
- "Sam, building KithNode" = named-but-faceless founder voice for build-in-public (Reddit now, LinkedIn later). Real name + story, zero face/video.

## Active channels (Phase 0, ~5 hr/wk)
- **Reddit (~2.5 hr/wk):** value comments in r/FinancialCareers, r/consulting, school subs; build-in-public posts (named founder) in r/EntrepreneurRideAlong, r/startups. See `reddit-playbook.md`. Funnel: bio link + "happy to share the template" to DM to waitlist.
- **Faceless TikTok (~2.5 hr/wk):** pillars = educational 40%, build-in-public faceless 30%, aesthetic/motivation 30%. Cross-post to Reels + Shorts. See `tiktok-scripts.md`. Funnel: bio link + pinned comment + on-screen CTA.

## The engine
- One content calendar (`content-calendar.md`).
- Repurposing loop: 1 idea from the want-vs-hate research becomes 1 Reddit value post + 1-2 TikTok scripts + (later) 1 LinkedIn post.
- Capture: `/waitlist` page + a lead magnet ("Warm-Path Recruiting Playbook").
- Weekly batch: ~2-3 hr block, draft with the marketing skills.
- Track: `tracking-log.md`, double down on what converts.

## Roadmap
- **Phase 0 (now to beta):** Reddit + faceless TikTok. Warm accounts, build the engine + lead magnet, first ~50-100 waitlist. No hard selling.
- **Phase 1 (beta):** add LinkedIn (named founder demos + build-in-public, also career capital); referral loop (founding members invite, skip the line); invite waitlist to beta.
- **Phase 2 (launch):** Product Hunt / BetaList; full launch sequence; intern takes IG + scheduling; convert waitlist to founding members (revenue KPI).

## Intern handoff
- Intern owns: TikTok production + scheduling, IG, calendar mechanics.
- Sam keeps: Reddit (founder voice), strategy, build-in-public narrative.
