## 4th format (evergreen): green-screen Reddit post

## Production kit
- Screen-record KithNode (CapCut screen capture or QuickTime). Hero asset.
- B-roll: Pexels / Pixabay (free): "city skyline," "laptop typing," "clock."
- Voice: your own voiceover (no face) is most authentic; or CapCut TTS; or captions-only over a trending sound.
- Captions: CapCut auto-captions, 2 lines max, highlight the key word.
- 9:16, hook in second one (text + motion + line all hit at once). Post over perfect.
