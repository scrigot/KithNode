## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Find a warm path into every firm on your target list. (Internally: "the network for people who don't have one.")
**What it does:** KithNode maps every alum at the firms a student is targeting, ranks each warm path by shared signals (school, club, Greek org, hometown, prior firm), and drafts authentic outreach the student edits and sends. It is an auto-maintaining CRM, so follow-ups never silently drop and no message reads as AI.
**Product category:** warm-path networking intelligence / personal recruiting CRM. (How they search: "networking tracker," "recruiting CRM," "alumni finder.")
**Product type:** Web SaaS, pre-launch (private alpha).
**Business model:** Generous free tier + one flat, transparent subscription (monthly $15, annual $120 = $10/mo). No credits, one-click cancel.

## Target Audience
**Target "companies" (the segment):** high-ambition undergrads breaking into IB, PE, and consulting. UNC plus target / semi-target / non-target schools (Wharton, Stern, Booth, Georgetown, Duke, etc.).
**Decision-makers:** the student is both user and buyer. Gen Z, acutely price-sensitive (87% report subscription fatigue, 60% cancel over a $5 increase).
**Primary use case:** manufacture the warm-intro advantage gatekeepers reserve for insiders, and never drop a follow-up.
**Jobs to be done:**
- "Find who I actually know (or could reach) at my target firms, and who to contact first."
- "Make sure I follow up, so I don't lose the replies follow-ups generate."
- "Write outreach that sounds like me, not a bot, so I don't torch my reputation."
**Use cases:** building a target-firm contact list, ranking warm paths, drafting coffee-chat outreach, tracking the pipeline through the recruiting cycle, prepping for chats.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** students with no insider network grind 100-200 cold emails, get ghosted, and their tracker silently rots, so they drop the follow-ups that generate ~42% of replies.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Spreadsheets / Notion: never tell you who's overdue, manual upkeep, blank cold-start.
- Folk / Clay: deal-shaped overkill, "a second job," credit-metered, invasive Gmail permissions.
- AI ghostwriters: produce slop that damages the sender's own reputation.
**What it costs them:** lost offers, wasted hours, damaged sender reputation, burnout ("post-recruitment depression").
**Emotional tension:** feeling fake or transactional (the "networking ick"), the paralyzing referral "ask," the ambiguity of being ghosted, comparison to peers' LinkedIn offers.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** spreadsheets / Notion (the real incumbent), falls short because it never nudges, rots over time, and cold-starts blank.
**Direct:** Folk, Clay / Mesh, fall short because they are deal/RevOps-shaped, credit-metered, and feel like a second job.
**Secondary:** Apollo, ContactOut, RocketReach (email finders), fall short on stale "verified" data, credit pricing, and bounce-reputation risk.
**Indirect:** alumni portals (Graduway/Hivebrite/PeopleGrove), ~5% sign-up and data rot; WSO Academy ($6k coaching), draws "the info is free" pushback.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Warm-path scoring (alma mater > recency/seniority > hometown/club/sport/prior-firm), ranked by referral power (line analyst above HR).
- AutoGuard human-in-the-loop: never auto-sends; AI is research, editor, and logistics, never the visible voice.
- AI-tell linter: cliché blacklist, no em-dash spam, no fake "I noticed you posted," register shifts by contact seniority.
- Auto-import + auto-enrich so the database is never blank; a "due today" follow-up engine.
- Confidence and freshness badge on every contact; flat transparent pricing, one-click cancel.
**How we do it differently:** surface the real signal as research the human converts into a genuine reason. Never spray-and-pray.
**Why that's better:** warm intros reply ~5x more than cold; authenticity is the moat against the AI-slop "poison the well" collapse.
**Why customers choose us:** it is the auto-maintaining warm-path CRM that kills the spreadsheet's only failure mode and never reads as AI.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Isn't this just AI spam?" | AutoGuard never auto-sends. AI only researches and drafts; you edit and send every word. |
| "Do I have to give my LinkedIn login or a password?" | No login, password, or extension required. Built on permitted public data. |
| "Another tool that'll rot like my spreadsheet?" | Auto-import + auto-enrich on day one, plus a "due today" engine so it maintains itself. |

**Anti-persona:** anyone who wants to blast hundreds of automated cold emails. KithNode deliberately caps touches and blocks auto-send.
